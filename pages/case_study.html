Case study

A case study in UX is a detailed account of a design project that explains the problem, the research and design process, and the final solution, highlighting how user needs shaped the outcome.
Below are two case studies from my projects that illustrate the design processes I follow. Please review them to gain insight into my approach.

Project 1

Next Generation Client xperience

This is a Wealth account website. It helps the clients to view their account informations.The system supports personalized investment strategies aligned with client risk profiles and goals. Automated reporting ensures regulatory compliance and transparent client communication.

Project 2

DXU - Call Scheduling Application

This is Dashboard interface created for scheduling meeting between clients. The system automatically assigns calls, syncs with internal calendars, and sends reminders to both parties. Missed appointments are reduced through automated follow-ups and rescheduling options.
